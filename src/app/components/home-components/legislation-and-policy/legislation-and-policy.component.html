Policy Initiatives

For the holistic development of the women's socio economic status, the organisation has been implementing the world's largest and most unique and outreach programme of  The Support to Training and Employment Programme for Women (STEP) providing a package of employability skills in various sectors comprising of Agriculture, Horticulture, Food Processing, Handlooms, Tailoring, Stitching, Embroidery, Zari etc. Handicrafts, Computer & IT enable services along with soft skills and skills for the workplace such as spoken English, Gems &Jewellery etc. There is effective coordination and monitoring of various sectoral programmes. Most of the programmes of the organisation are run through non-governmental organisations. Efforts are made to have more effective involvement of NGOs. The major policy initiatives undertaken by the Ministry in the recent past include universalization of STEP and Sukanya Samridhi Yojna, launching a funding programme for girl child, for protection of Child Rights and enactment of Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act.
